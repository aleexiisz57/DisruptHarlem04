H.B.A.

WHO WE ARE.


We believe local businesses build stronger communities.

Our 36-year history of serving Harlem continues to transform small businesses into viable economic agents. We strive to inspire new ways of thinking about what’s possible for our entrepreneurs. Our services include running Harlem’s first co-working space, teaching under-served youth how to code and start their own tech company, and offering free business consulting and workshops throughout the neighborhood.
