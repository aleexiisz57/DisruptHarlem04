I am a resident of the Bronx in New York City but i lived in Harlem for a couple of years growing up, i have seen how it's slowly changing with new people coming in, new stores and restaurants have opened it's starting to look so different from when i first arrived. But growing up i also saw the not so good side of Harlem and i always felt like i wanted to do something to change it and not just make it better for me but also for the people around me, so i decided to learn software engineering to find ways to use technology to make not just Harlem better but reach out to other parts of the world and help as well.
